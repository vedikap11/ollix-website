Philosophy

Intelligence
without
surveillance.

Indian schools are data-rich and insight-poor. Ollix is built around one constraint: the institution learns, the individual stays private.

i.
Anonymisation by default
All data is aggregated before any insight surfaces. No administrator ever sees a named student attached to a risk flag. The pattern, not the person.
ii.
Patterns over profiles
Ollix models cohort behaviour, not individual deviation. The unit of intelligence is the group — never the child.
iii.
Action-oriented signals
Every insight maps to an intervention. Ollix doesn't generate reports — it surfaces decision points for school leadership at the moment they matter.
iv.
Built for India's scale
Priced in INR, compliant with DPDPA, and designed for the operational reality of under-resourced admin teams across Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.

Product

Four tools.
One clear picture.

01
Cohort intelligence dashboard
Real-time engagement and risk visibility across your entire school. Attendance trends, flag counts, engagement index — all aggregated, all anonymised. No spreadsheets. No roster diving. Just the signal that matters.
02
School Health Score™
A composite institutional score updated weekly — tracking retention risk, learning momentum, attendance, and operational health. The number your board actually needs to see.
03
Early warning system
Ollix models leading indicators of disengagement, not lagging ones. By the time a student drops out or a class underperforms, the window has closed. The warning system gives you 6–8 weeks of runway to act.
04
Indian Education Index
Ollix's flagship annual publication — anonymised benchmark data from partner schools across India. Your School Health Score in national context. The report that earns trust with parents and boards alike.
